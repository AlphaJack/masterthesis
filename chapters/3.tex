\section{Tables}
\label{tables}

To add a table:


\begin{outerTable}
 \caption{A tiny table}
 \label{widetable}
 \begin{innerTable}{colspec={Xlr}}
  Head A  & Head B  & Head C \\
  Cell A1 & Cell B1 &     10 \\
  Cell A2 & Cell B2 &   1000 \\
 \end{innerTable}
\end{outerTable}


To reference a cell or row in a table:


\begin{outerTable}
 \caption[Short name]{Long description of the table}
 \label{widetablewithreferences}
 \begin{innerTable}{colspec={lX}}
  Company & Industry \\
  \iddef{C1} & Consulting \\
  \iddef{C2} & Fashion manufacturing \\
 \end{innerTable}
\end{outerTable}
